Cooking

While I'm no professional chef, one of my favorite pastimes is to experiment in the kitchen. I love trying new recipies and experimenting with new flavors. Some of my favorite recipies are linked below:

Butternut Squash Pasta Sauce
This butternut squash pasta sauce is one of my favorite fall recipies (but honestly, I'll make it year round!). It's a fun twist on typical pasta sacue, and pairs well with grilled or roasted chicken for some extra protein.
Cilantro Lime Skillet Chicken
Cilantro-Lime is a fantastic flavor combination, and when paired with chicken, some rice, and maybe a little guacamole and tortilla chips, it makes the perfect lunch or dinnner.
Black Bean Burgers
Vegetarian, but still want a good burger? No worries! I love making this black bean and carrot burger when I don't have any meat in the house, or just feel like something a little different.
